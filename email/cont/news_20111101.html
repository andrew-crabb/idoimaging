New Mail Format

If you've received email updates before you'll certainly notice that the format has changed. Emails are now in full HTML and we're using an email provider to make subscribing and unsubscribing easier. We're adding a short section 'Notable Releases', highlighting some significant updates that have been released since the last email, and a sidebar with a quick mention of all the projects that have released a new version in the same period. This is a good way to find great programs that you may not have known about. The 'Notable Releases' and sidebar content are archived in the I Do Imaging Blog, so if you don't get an email because none of the projects you follow have released an update, you can read the updates there.

The updates you receive still contain the updates on programs you are interested in and selected to follow.
